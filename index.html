About Me

My name is Catherine Joy Calupas and I am a 19-year-old Junior Game Developer based in Vienna, Austria. I graduated 2020 with excellence at the HTL Spengergasse (Technical College in Vienna) in Game Design.

The most exciting aspect of developing a game is testing it. Of course, you have to test every line of code itself but playing through the whole thing without game-breaking bugs always leaves me satisfied.

My biggest accomplishment is winning the SUBOTRON Students Live Pitch 2020 together with my diploma project team. I pitched our game in front of an online audience consisting of the jury, other competing projects and interested viewers all around Europe.

I am currently looking for work in the game industry. Preferably as a developer/programmer but I am also interested in Game Design and Art. If you want to know more about me (CV, certificates etc.) please send me a message to cat.calupas@gmail.com!
